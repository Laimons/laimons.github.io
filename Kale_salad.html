Kale salad

Products you will need :

  • Kale 500g
  • Yeast flakes 50g
  • Lemon 1
  • Fried onion flakes 100g
  • Fried garlic flakes 50g
  • Black garlic 10g
  • olive oil 1tbs
  • Maldon salt
  • Red Peppers
  • Papaja

How to make them

Take leaves of kale from branches and even them out on board, then take meat hammer and bash them for a bit so they become soft. Put black garlic in mortar and pestle and mix them together. Peel the papaya and shave it in different and not yet big sizes using vegetable peeler. Then add Kale in bowl, Yeast flakes, Lemon juice of half a lemon, fried onion and garlic flakes, black garlic oil, touch of maldon salt and crushed red peppers and shaved papaya. Put on plates and enjoy.
