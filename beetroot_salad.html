Salads with caramalized yellow beetroots, caramilized nuts and brie cheese for 2 people

Products you will need :

  • Salad mix 200g
  • Yellow beetroots (red ones works as well) 2 pieces
  • Brie cheese 100 g
  • Hazel nuts 100g
  • Salt 300 g
  • Honey 1 tbs
  • Sugar 30 g
  • Slice of bread
  • Butter
  • Lemon juice 10g
  • Olive oil 20g

Equipment you will need

  • Knife
  • Salad spinner or clean rag
  • Pan
  • Bowl
  • Pot
  • Oven pan

How to make them.


Preheat oven to 180 C. Take oven pan and pour salt evenly and put your washed beetroots (do not peel them) in salt and bake them for 1h30min or until they are soft. Wash your salads and rinse them off using salad spinner or just put them on clean rag and let them dry a bit. After that caramalize sugar in small pot on medium heat (temperature of sugar has to reach 183 degrees celcius or 362 degrees Fahrenheit, then drop in the pot hazzelnuts and stir it for one minute then put the heat off and put the pot asside. When nuts are cooled down smash them in smaller pieces. Now for the dressing. Pour lemon juice an dolive oil together, touch of salt, sugar and black pepper. Mix it together. After beetroots are backed peel them and cut them in chunks. Also cut brie cheese in similay chunks as beetroots. Drop dried salads in bowl and add salad dressing, mix them up. Place salads on plate, dash some caramilized hazelnuts, place beetroots on top together with brie cheese. Toast loaf of bread and put some butter on it. Enjoy.
