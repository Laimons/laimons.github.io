Potato salad for 7 persons

Products you will need :

  • Boiled sausege 300g
  • Marinated cucumber 300g
  • Potatoes 700g
  • Shallot onions 100g
  • Parsley 100 g
  • 1 egg
  • 250-300g oil
  • 1/4 lemon
  • Dijon mustard 1tbs,1tbs
  • Red Peppers
  • Salt

How to make them

Wash potatos and start to boil them in cold water with touch of salt. Cut sausage approximatley in 0.5cmx 0.5cm cubes. Take cucumbers and cut them in circles half centemitre wide, put them in a bowl. Peel shallot onions and cut them in 4 parts, than cut them in similar size as cucumber and put them in same bowl. Take leaves of the parsley and roll them togetcher then chop them and yes put them in same bowl as well. When potatos are soft rinse them off and place them to cool down. When they are cool, peel them and cut in similar chunks as other products. Now lets make aljoli. Place whole egg in seperate bowl, add 1tbs mustard, pinch of redd peppers and salt, squeeze in juice from 1/4 lemon. Now whisk it together, and slowly add oil, it should be dripping in egg not like pouring and whisk it all the time (yes super slowly while you are whisking and call for help or just place wet towel under the bowl). When aljoli seems to thicken and it holds together nicely add salt to your taste and you can add some siracha or other herbs if you wish. Now put aljoli together with cut products and whoala salad is done, you can add extra mustard, garlic if you like. Enjoy.
